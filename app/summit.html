Blockstack
Summit 2015

The Open Source Blockchain Community

September 12, 2015 - 9am-5pm

D'Agostino Hall, NYU, 108 W 3rd St, New York, NY

Register for Blockstack Summit

We are excited to announce and invite you to join us at our first community event, Blockstack Summit!

Scheduled for September 12th in New York City, Blockstack Summit is an all-day workshop where attendees will be able to have high quality discussions and get real work done throughout the day alongside some of the most talented and influential developers working with decentralized applications and blockchain technology today. If you go to just one blockchain event for the rest of this year, let it be Blockstack Summit!

Attendees should bring their ideas, problems, and questions about decentralized application development so that we can all learn and benefit from the growing volume of wisdom in this burgeoning community. The workshop will be following the “unconference” format, so all participants will have a chance to have their voices heard and ideas discussed.

Possible topics of discussion include: distributed hash tables, blockchain scalability, identity, messaging protocols, smart contracts, real-time collaboration, storage systems, reputation, and voting.

To close out the inaugural Blockstack Summit, we are honored to be hosting a Q&A panel with four visionary leaders with deep experience working with developers of disruptive technology. This is not a discussion to be missed.

Albert Wenger, Partner at Union Square Ventures (USV)
Jalak Jobanputra, Founder of Future/Perfect Ventures
Naval Ravikant, Co-founder AngelList & Angel Investor
William Mougayar, Angel Investor

Tickets for Blockstack Summit are limited, so if you’re interested in attending, register today to reserve your spot.
